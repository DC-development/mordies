Services:

Mordies.com offers services the following: translations, interpretation and language training.

Interpretation

Interpretation:

Interpretation is the action of translating word from one language to another. Two modes of interpretation, consecutive and simultaneous, are known and applied as fit. Contact us with your inquiries regarding interpretation needs.

Translation

Translation

Whether you are looking for someone to translate your project related documents from one language to another, or you plan to take your business abroad, be it through exports or a subsidiary, you may find you need the support of language professionals to help you with your endeavor. Whilst basic knowledge of the target language is a good starting point to pursue translation, with certain texts a thorough knowledge of the target culture or expertise in specialized fields is necessary to render a sound translation. Whether a few lines of text found in a notebook on your attic or the translation of your company website or latest novel; no task is too minor nor are any too complex; we gladly will make you a fair offer, or respond as to whether we can meet your budget. Ready to discuss your translation requirements with us? Simply leave a message and we will get back to you.

Foreign Language Training

Foreign Language Training

Whether you need training for specific purposes such as career opportunities, international relationships, to access news compiled in a particular language, to train your staff, to reactivate knowledge you did not recently have the opportunity to make much use of or simply because you enjoy learning, contact us regarding such matters. We will help you on your quest to finding the right program, teacher, presenter or school to make the vision you have for you or your company reality without overstretching your budget, and do guarantee your satisfaction. New classes start on regular basis.
